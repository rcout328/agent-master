# Competitor Analysis for amazon

## TOP COMPETITORS
Walmart: Leading US e-commerce competitor with a large network of physical stores offering convenient in-store pickup and competitive pricing.

eBay: Major e-commerce platform offering a peer-to-peer marketplace with auctions and fixed-price listings, catering to a wide variety of products.

Alibaba: Dominant force in global e-commerce, particularly in China, with various subsidiaries (AliExpress, Taobao, Tmall) offering B2B, B2C, and C2C models.

Target: Significant US retailer with a loyal customer base, leveraging its physical stores for online order pickup and returns, and increasingly focusing on online sales.

Flipkart: Leading online e-commerce platform in India, competing directly with Amazon India, with a focus on fast-growing markets.
