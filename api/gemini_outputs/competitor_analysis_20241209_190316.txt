# Competitor Analysis for amazon

## TOP COMPETITORS
Walmart: Leading US e-commerce competitor; leverages its vast network of physical stores for in-store pickup, easy returns, and grocery delivery, offering competitive pricing.

eBay: Major e-commerce platform; differentiates itself with an auction format alongside fixed-price listings, providing a large marketplace for diverse products.

Alibaba: Dominant e-commerce player in China; comprises AliExpress, Taobao, and Tmall, offering B2B, B2C, and C2C models with a wide product selection.

Target: Strong US competitor; maintains a loyal customer base with convenient shopping experiences and now offers same-day delivery and online order pickup.

Flipkart: Leading e-commerce platform in India; competes with Amazon's Indian operations with a similar business model but features a unique loyalty program.
