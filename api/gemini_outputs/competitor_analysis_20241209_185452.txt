# Competitor Analysis for amazon

## TOP COMPETITORS
Walmart: Leading US e-commerce competitor with a strong physical store presence, offering convenient in-store pickup and competitive pricing.

eBay: Major global e-commerce platform known for its auction-style listings and diverse product range, serving both B2C and C2C markets.

Alibaba: Dominant e-commerce player in China, comprising multiple platforms (AliExpress, Taobao, Tmall) offering a wide variety of products at competitive prices.

Target: Significant US retailer with a loyal customer base, increasingly competing in e-commerce with same-day delivery and convenient in-store pickup options.

Flipkart: Leading e-commerce platform in India, offering a vast selection of products and a unique loyalty program, challenging Amazon's dominance in the region.
